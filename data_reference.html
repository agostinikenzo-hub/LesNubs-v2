Les Nübs — CSV Data Reference

Overview of all columns currently exported from fetch_riot_data.py and their meaning in the League of Legends context.

1️⃣ Metadata & Game Info

ColumnMeaning
Game #Internal counter for unique matches recorded.
DateMatch start time in UTC.
TeamConstant “Les Nübs”. Identifies your team.
SeasonCompetitive season (e.g., 2025).
SplitEvent or internal split name (e.g., Fall Split).
Match IDUnique Riot match identifier (for traceability).
Queue TypeRiot queue ID (e.g., 440 = Ranked Flex).
Game ModeGame mode such as CLASSIC, ARAM, URF.
Map IDMap code (11 = Summoner’s Rift).
Game VersionPatch version (e.g., 14.19).
Winning Team100 = Blue side win, 200 = Red side win.

2️⃣ Player Identity & Role

ColumnMeaning
PlayerRiot ID of the player in this match.
ROLETeam position (TOP, JUNGLE, MIDDLE, ADC, SUPPORT).
ChampionChampion played by the player.
Champion LevelFinal champion level at match end.
Summoner Spell 1 / 2IDs of the two summoner spells used.

3️⃣ Match Result & Performance

ColumnMeaning
ResultWin or Loss for the player’s team.
Kills / Deaths / AssistsCore combat stats.
KDAKill–Death–Assist ratio (to be computed later).
Kill Part %Kill participation relative to team kills.
MVP / ACE / OP.GG ScorePlaceholders for custom metrics.
TIMEMatch duration (minutes).
Gold Earned / SpentTotal gold earned and used.
XP/minAverage experience per minute.
XP Diff @10XP difference at 10 minutes vs. lane opponent.
CS / CS/minMinions + neutral monsters killed, and per minute rate.
CS Diff @10CS difference vs. lane opponent at 10 minutes.

4️⃣ Vision & Map Control

ColumnMeaning
Vision ScoreOverall vision impact metric from Riot.
WARDSTotal wards placed.
WARDS KILLEDEnemy wards destroyed.
PINKControl (pink) wards purchased.
Crowd Control Time DealtTotal seconds spent applying CC.
CC ScoreDuplicate metric from Riot’s challenges data.

5️⃣ Damage & Fighting Stats

ColumnMeaning
Damage DealtTotal damage dealt to enemy champions.
Damage TakenTotal damage received from enemies.
Damage to ObjectivesDamage to dragons, barons, heralds.
Damage to TurretsDamage dealt to structures.
Team Damage %Share of total team damage to champions.
Total HealTotal HP restored by healing effects.
Total ShieldAmount of shielding applied to allies.
Solo / Double / Triple / Quadra / Penta KillsMulti-kill and solo-kill stats.
Damage TimelinePlaceholder for future timeline integration.

6️⃣ Team Objective Stats

ColumnMeaning
First BloodWhether the team secured the game’s first kill.
First TowerWhether the team destroyed the first turret.
First BaronWhether the team killed the first Baron Nashor.
First DragonWhether the team took the first dragon.
Dragon / Baron / Herald KillsTotal number of these objectives secured.
Tower / Inhibitor KillsTotal structures destroyed by the team.
